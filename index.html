Welcome to my personal webpage! I am a Ph.D. candidate at the MMAI Lab at KAIST, under the supervision of Professor Joon Son Chung. I earned my Bachelor’s degree also from KAIST (Feb. 2023), and my academic journey has been deeply rooted in the field of multimodal communication, particularly focusing on video and speech interactions.

My current research interests are in the development of artificial intelligence technologies for the deaf community, encompassing sign language recognition and translation. Additionally, I am exploring innovative areas such as talking head synthesis and voice generation technologies. These endeavors aim to bridge communication gaps and enhance interaction through cutting-edge AI advancements. Join me as I navigate through these exciting challenges and breakthroughs in multimodal AI.
